•	The study duration consists of 4 levels (years). 

•	Each level contains: 

o	Fall Semester 

o	Spring Semester

•	Summer semester ≥ 6 weeks

•	Total required credits: 140 credit hours 

•	Minimum cumulative GPA for graduation: 2.00 (Grade D) 

•	A student passes a course if: 

o	Score ≥ 60% 

o	Grade ≥ D 

Special Courses

•	ASU101 and INCO 102: 

o	Not included in GPA

3\. Academic Load Constraints

Regular Semester

•	Minimum load: 12 credit hours 

•	Maximum load: 19 credit hours 

•	Can reach 21 hours if: 

o	Previous Semester GPA ≥ 3.0 

o	OR student is in final semester 

•	Academic probation students: Max: 12 credit hours

•	If a student’s current academic level is lower than the expected level based on their admission year, they are considered academically delayed.

In this case, the maximum allowed credit hours for the semester is limited to 16 credit hours.

Summer Semester

•	Maximum load: 6 credit hours

9\. Course Retake Rules

41\. Retake Eligibility Update

Students are allowed to retake courses with the following grades:

•	F (Fail) 

•	Abs (Absent) 

•	W (Withdrawn) 

•	I (Incomplete) 

Rule:

Any course with grade in {F, Abs, W, I} can be retaken.

✔️ Notes:

•	Retaken courses follow normal GPA rules 

•	Latest grade also counts in GPA 

•	Max retake limit: 

o	12 credit hours 

•	After limit: 

o	Grade capped at D

10\. GPA Calculation

Quality Points

Quality Points = Course Credits × Grade Points

Semester GPA

Sum(Quality Points) / Sum(Credits)

Cumulative GPA

Total Quality Points / Total Credits
